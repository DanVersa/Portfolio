Why Do I Code?

It's really unlike anything else. I can create anything I want out of a bunch of numbers and letters that are put into a specific order. I have unlimited resources to build whatever I want and I just have to imagine it and code. It's like having a superpower (If I was a superhero, my name would be ElDano just so you know, it has a nice ring to it). I can build something out of thin air for millions of people! However, one of the biggest reasons I love coding is the sense of community, collaboration, and helping other people. Sometimes I spend hours on the freeCodeCamp chat just answering coding questions because I love people's reactions when they have that Ah-Ha moment. I love going to coding meetups and hackathons in order to meet people who are passionate about coding just as I am. And I love going to Tim Hortons because I love their muffins. In the future, I want to make something that will help people and give them hope by using code. Code is something I hold near and dear to my heart

-Dan
