Hey there! My name's Dan and I've been designing brands & websites since middle school. Back in 2017, I realized that I didn't have to wait to get a college degree in order to start in my industry. So, with an enthusiasm to learn more, I started my own brand design business, Dan V Design Co.


Since then, I've gotten the chance to work with a custom shoe designer empowering women, a luxury cleaning company that got to clean Katy Perry's dress, and much more. Now, I'm hoping to take the business on the road as I hike, climb, surf, and design around the country!
